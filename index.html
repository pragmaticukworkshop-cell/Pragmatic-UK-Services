THE PROCESS

From enquiry to espresso — how it works

Simple, professional, and fast. Here's what to expect when you work with us.

1

Get in Touch

Fill in our enquiry form or call us directly. Tell us about your machine, your issue, and your location — and upload photos or video of the fault to help us assess it straight away.

2

We Assess & Quote

We'll review your images or video and diagnose the fault remotely where possible. In some cases we can walk you through a fix over the phone — saving you time and a call-out fee. If a visit is needed, we'll provide a clear, no-obligation quote before any work starts.

3

Engineer Attends

Our engineer visits your site or collects your machine for workshop service — fully stocked and ready to fix.

4

Back Up & Running

Machine returned or signed off on-site, with a full service report and follow-up support included.
